# Changelog

## 2026-07-14

### Link/Unlink CAS
Users can now Link/Unlink their CAS account to feely switch between either authentication method.

### Information Reports
Users can now directly report wrong information on a course page.

### Whispr Reviews
The reviews from Whispr were ported on to grapevine and are visible as Archive Reviews. They do not affect the average ratings.

## 2026-07-13

### Adjustable Overall scores
Users now have the option to adjust their overall scores instead of them just being an average of all the axes.

### Sorting
Improved sorting on courses and faculty pages.

## 2026-07-12

### Grapevine Public Release 
Initial public release of grapevine, 5 days before course registration for the Monsoon 2026 semester. Access is restricted to the internal network.
